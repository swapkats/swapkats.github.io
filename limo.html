limo

limo (now h2o) is a startup I've worked with from inception. In short it was uber for commute. Challenge was to build a truly seamless commute experience for office commuters in mumbai where commute is hell.

In this project I've tried my hands on design, because why not? I worked closely on product design. I've always was an admirer of good products and I've been told many times I have good sense of design. So I worked on both design and frontend for this project.

Because I was very quick with frontend development, I managed to do design as well. This made limo's design process very lean and agile. Their time to quickly iterate ideas to production improved significantly.
